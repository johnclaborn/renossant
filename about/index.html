About

Renossant Inc. is a music production and publishing label founded by John Claborn in May of 2018. Our team prides themselves by being very hands on when it comes to production, recording, mixing and mastering as everything is done in house. We attract artists and labels who are looking for a one stop shop team to elevate their music career.

John grew up around music and entertainment his entire life. At two years old, he would get into everything electronic, messing around with devices with lights and buttons made for adults. His parents eventually got him a Fisher Price vinyl record player of his own and he spun records from Michael Jackson, Stevie Wonder, and Prince, to The Wiz and Flash Dance Soundtracks. His curiosity for the sounds in music and the people that created them led him to pursue band in middle school and eventually modern music production.

After dropping out of college in 2010, John went to work with local music labels and collaborators, continuing to make music, record, produce and mix. Fast forward to now and, after many hours in the studio, John has amassed an impressive list of clientele and credentials including:

​Ariana Grande, Timbaland, Wiz Khalifa, Styles P, Paul Wall, Killer Mike, Ty Dolla$, Sticky Fingaz, Xzibit, King Los, Lyrica Anderson, Chamillionaire, E-40, Shawty Lo, Stalley, Shella da Blasian, Wacka Flocka, Yung Nation, Martellus Bennett, Cardo, DJ Mustard, Starley and many others.
